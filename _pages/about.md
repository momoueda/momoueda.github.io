Momoko Ueda is a PhD student at Simon Fraser University School of Criminology and conducts research at the Centre for Forensic Research. Her research interest includes stable isotope analysis of human tissues for forensic investigations. She obtained her BSc from Univeristy of British Columbia in General Life Sciences while also specializing in Chemistry and Earth/Ocean Sciences and an MA from Simon Fraser University.
